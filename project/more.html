Hooks

You might need different fish hooks to catch fish in different size. Hook sizes range from number 32 (smallest) to 19/0 (largest).

You can install hooks on lures or put baits on the hooks.

Lures

Fishing lures are usually artificial baits that are designed to mimic real fish in order to get the attention of predators.

Baits

Different types of fish are attracted to different types of food. Bait is what you place on the hooks to attract the fish. They can be worms, small fish, or bread.
