Spinning reels

Spinning reels hang under the rods, and lines wrap around the moving spool.

Casting reels

Casting reels rest on the top of the rods with a trigger handle.

Fly fishing reels

Fly fishing reels sit underneath the rods and look just like simple line holders. They help belance the fly rods to perform smoothly.

Reel Type Advantages Disadvantages
Spinning Reel -Easy to cast
-No backlash
-Works well with light lures
 -Line twist
Casting Reel -Can handle bigger fish
-Works well with large lures
-Strong drag system
 -Difficult to cast
-Backlash may occur
