Spinning reels

Spinning reels hang under the rods, and lines wrap around the moving spool.

The good things about spining reels is that they are easy to cast, no backlash, and works well with light lures, but they can get line twist sometimes.

Casting reels

Casting reels rest on the top of the rods with a trigger handle.

The good things about casting reels is that they can handle big fish, have strong drag system, and works well with heavy lures, but they are difficult to cast.

Fly fishing reels

Fly fishing reels sit underneath the rods and look just like simple line holders. They help belance the fly rods to perform smoothly.
